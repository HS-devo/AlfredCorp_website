Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

At Alfred Corp., accessible from alfredcorp.com and associated platforms, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Alfred Corp. and how we use it to provide supervised agentic corporate services for Canadian entrepreneurs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you directly provide, such as when you request an invite, sign up for waitlists, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Contact information (such as name, email address, physical address, and phone number).
  • Business metadata (company name, industry, roles, and connected workspace platforms).
  • Content of communications, review notes, and requested task definitions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways to power and safeguard our platform, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Alfred Corp supervised agent network.
  • Analyze and verify workflow results via our human-in-the-loop professional review.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our platform to develop custom deployments.
  • Communicate with you for customer service, product updates, and system verification.
  • Detect, prevent, and mitigate fraud, technical issues, and system compliance errors.

3. Log Files

Alfred Corp follows a standard procedure of using system log files. These logs record activities when visitors interact with our interfaces. This includes IP addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISP), date/time stamps, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, auditing AI actions, and securing workspaces.

4. Data Custody & Security

The security of your database, assets, and business data is paramount. We implement strict transport layer security (TLS 1.3), end-to-end encrypted databases, and clean sandbox virtual environments for custom automation execution. No automated task output is ever finalized without professional security reviews or proper access control guidelines in place.

5. CCPA and PIPEDA Compliance

We recognize the high privacy standards expected by Canadian and international clients under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). We ensure:

  • Your consent is obtained for the collection, use, or disclosure of personal information.
  • Personal information is safeguarded with robust physical, organizational, and technological security measures.
  • You hold full access rights to review, rectify, or purge any stored business details under our custody.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@alfredcorp.com.
